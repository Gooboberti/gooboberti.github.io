THE CREATOR

Building at the intersection
of play, creation & emergence.

I'm Gooboberti — a full-time livestreamer and multi-disciplinary creator. I spend 12+ hours a day deep in immersive survival and crafting worlds (No Man's Sky, Crimson Desert and beyond), composing original music and anthems with AI tools like Suno, crafting cinematic visuals and viral shorts with Grok Imagine, and developing Modular Life Sim — an ambitious open-ended evolutionary simulation exploring persistent identity, collective behavior, and the conditions for higher-order agency.

This is more than content. It's an ongoing experiment in consciousness, systems, and what happens when complexity is allowed to stack across multiple layers — from individual creatures with evolvable modules and neural architectures, to MacroOrganisms with internal ecosystems, to the potential emergence of true collective intelligence.
